Final Project Proposal - Kombucha Review Page

Topic

My website will be a kombucha review page. I started drinking kombucha over 4 years ago and have tried many different brands, some of which I am very passionate about. I want my page to showcase my favorite brands of kombucha, the flavor profiles, and the benefits of drinking kombucha.

Audience

The audience for my website includes anyone that enjoys kombucha or is interested in trying it. Whether you are a skeptic or a lover, there is a kombucha for you!

Design & Style

The design of the website will be simple, but bright, just like the flavor of kombucha. I want it to appear similar to the way that kombucha and other probiotic drinks are marketed. I will use simple design techniques and a bright color profile to accomplish this.

Inspired by:

  • Poppi
  • Health Ade Kombucha
  • Humm Kombucha

Scope

The website will contain 3 pages. The first will be an overview of the benefits of kombucha and some popular brands. The second page will be a gallery of all the different kombuchas I have tried. The final page will be a rating of the top 5 kombuchas I have tried including photos.
